Maternity

Overview

Eka Hospital's Maternity department provides comprehensive care and support for expectant mothers throughout their pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum period. Our team of obstetricians, midwives, and support staff are committed to ensuring a safe and positive experience for both mother and baby.

Features:

  1. Antenatal Care
  2. Labour and Delivery Services
  3. Postpartum Care
  4. Newborn Care
  5. Breastfeeding Support
  6. Parenting Classes
  7. High-Risk Pregnancy Management
  8. Ultrasound Services
  9. Childbirth Education

Treatment:

  1. Antenatal Care: Regular check-ups and screenings to monitor the health of the mother and baby during pregnancy.
  2. Labour and Delivery Services: Support and assistance during childbirth, including pain management options and medical interventions if needed.
  3. Postpartum Care: Monitoring and support for the mother's recovery after childbirth, including breastfeeding assistance and emotional support.
  4. Newborn Care: Medical care and assessments for newborn babies, including vaccinations and screenings for developmental milestones.
  5. Breastfeeding Support: Education and assistance for mothers to establish successful breastfeeding and address any challenges.
  6. Parenting Classes: Education and guidance for expectant parents on topics such as infant care, safety, and nutrition.
  7. High-Risk Pregnancy Management: Specialized care and monitoring for pregnancies with complications or medical conditions.
  8. Ultrasound Services: Imaging scans to monitor fetal development and detect any abnormalities during pregnancy.
  9. Childbirth Education: Classes and workshops to prepare expectant parents for labor, delivery, and early parenthood.
