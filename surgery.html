Surgery

Overview

>Eka Hospital's Surgery department provides advanced surgical interventions and procedures for various medical conditions and diseases. Our team of highly skilled surgeons, anesthesiologists, and support staff is dedicated to ensuring optimal outcomes for every patient.

Features:

  1. Comprehensive Surgical Services
  2. State-of-the-Art Operating Theaters
  3. Specialized Surgical Units
  4. Postoperative Care
  5. Minimally Invasive Techniques
  6. Emergency Surgical Care
  7. Collaborative Approach to Care
  8. Advanced Surgical Equipment
  9. Patient Education and Support

Treatment:

  1. Comprehensive Surgical Services: A wide range of surgical procedures to address various medical conditions, including elective and emergency surgeries.
  2. State-of-the-Art Operating Theaters: Well-equipped operating rooms with advanced technology and instrumentation for safe and efficient surgical procedures.
  3. Specialized Surgical Units: Dedicated units for specific surgical specialties, such as orthopedic surgery, neurosurgery, and cardiovascular surgery.
  4. Postoperative Care: Monitoring and support for patients after surgery to ensure proper healing and recovery.
  5. Minimally Invasive Techniques: Surgical techniques that minimize trauma to the body, resulting in faster recovery times and reduced postoperative pain.
  6. Emergency Surgical Care: Prompt surgical intervention for acute medical conditions and traumatic injuries requiring urgent treatment.
  7. Collaborative Approach to Care: Multidisciplinary teams working together to provide comprehensive care tailored to each patient's needs.
  8. Advanced Surgical Equipment: State-of-the-art surgical instruments and technology to enhance surgical precision and outcomes.
  9. Patient Education and Support: Information and guidance for patients and their families before, during, and after surgery to promote understanding and compliance with treatment plans.
