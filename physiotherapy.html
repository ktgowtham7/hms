Physiotherapy

Overview

Eka Hospital's Physiotherapy department offers a wide range of services aimed at restoring function, improving mobility, and enhancing overall quality of life for patients recovering from injury, illness, or surgery.

Our team of experienced physiotherapists utilizes evidence-based techniques and personalized treatment plans to address each patient's unique needs and goals.

Features:

  1. Comprehensive Assessment and Evaluation
  2. Individualized Treatment Plans
  3. State-of-the-Art Rehabilitation Equipment
  4. Manual Therapy Techniques
  5. Therapeutic Exercise Programs
  6. Pain Management Strategies
  7. Balance and Gait Training
  8. Postural Correction
  9. Education and Home Exercise Programs

Treatment:

  1. Manual Therapy: Hands-on techniques to mobilize joints, improve flexibility, and reduce pain.
  2. Therapeutic Exercise: Customized exercise programs to strengthen muscles, improve endurance, and enhance functional capacity.
  3. Electrotherapy: Use of electrical modalities such as ultrasound, TENS, and electrical stimulation to manage pain and promote tissue healing.
  4. Joint Mobilization: Techniques to restore normal joint mechanics and improve range of motion.
  5. Soft Tissue Mobilization: Massage and stretching techniques to release muscle tension and improve tissue flexibility.
  6. Neuromuscular Re-education: Exercises and activities to improve coordination, balance, and proprioception.
  7. Gait Training: Rehabilitation exercises to improve walking pattern and restore normal gait mechanics.
  8. Postural Correction: Strategies to improve posture and prevent musculoskeletal dysfunction.
  9. Functional Training: Activities designed to simulate real-life movements and improve functional independence.
