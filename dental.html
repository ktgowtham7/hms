Dental Care

Overview

Eka Hospital offers comprehensive dental care services to patients of all ages. Our team of experienced dentists and specialists is dedicated to providing high-quality dental treatments in a comfortable and caring environment.

We understand the importance of oral health in overall well-being and strive to promote healthy smiles through preventive care, education, and advanced treatment options. Whether you need a routine check-up or complex dental surgery, we are here to meet your needs with personalized care and attention.

Feautures:

  1. Routine Dental Checkups and Cleanings
  2. Fillings and Restorative Dentistry
  3. Dental Implants
  4. Orthodontic Treatments (Braces and Invisalign)
  5. Root Canal Therapy
  6. Periodontal (Gum) Treatments
  7. Oral Surgery
  8. Teeth Whitening
  9. Pediatric Dentistry

Treatments:

  1. General Dentistry: Routine dental examinations, cleanings, fillings, and oral hygiene instructions for maintaining optimal oral health.
  2. Orthodontics: Corrects misaligned teeth and jaws using braces, aligners, or other orthodontic appliances to improve aesthetics and function.
  3. Endodontics: Treats infections or damage within the tooth's pulp (root canal therapy) to save the tooth and relieve pain.
  4. Periodontics: Focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of gum diseases, including scaling, root planing, and gum surgeries.
  5. Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery: Addresses complex dental and facial conditions, including tooth extractions, jaw surgeries, and facial trauma repair.
  6. Implant Dentistry: Restores missing teeth by surgically placing dental implants in the jawbone and attaching artificial teeth (crowns or bridges) to them.
  7. Pediatric Dentistry: Provides specialized dental care for children, including preventive treatments, early orthodontic interventions, and behavior management techniques.
