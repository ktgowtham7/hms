Cardiology

Overview

A highly skilled, experienced, and dedicated team of cardiologists, cardiothoracic surgeons, and cardiac anesthetists work on some of the most complex cardiac procedures both on adults and children at Eka Hospital. They are aptly supported by the state-of-the-art Coronary Care and post-surgical critical care Unit, which also has a nursing and paramedical team to handle any type of cardiac emergency round the clock. The follow-up of the patient is completed by the Cardiac Rehabilitation physiotherapy team, which is one of the first in the country. The department boasts two Cath-labs, of which one has 3D integration with image reconstruction support for advanced procedures like TAVI and LAAC. An average of around 20 cardiac procedures are performed at this unit every day.

Eka Hospital's Heart Centre performs various invasive procedures, and success rates are at par with global standards. Two state-of-the-art Digital Cath Labs are well supported by well-equipped Critical Care Units, while a team of doctors takes care of post-procedure patients. All modern facilities such as Artificial Ventilator, Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump (IABP), and Bedside Central Monitoring are available to ensure comprehensive cardiac care. The Centre is the first in the state to have installed an advanced 3D Electro Anatomical Mapping System used in the treatment of heart rhythm disorders and continues to lead from the front in adapting the latest technology in heart rhythm management, including contact force sensing-based ablation. It is one of the few centres in the country offering the entire spectrum of complex percutaneous intervention ranging from Rotablation to intravascular lithotripsy with coronary imaging guidance, including IVUS and OCT.

Treatment:

  1. Non Invasive Lad – 2D transthoracic and transesophageal echo, DSE, STE, Contrast Echo
  2. Interventional Cardiology – Cardiac catheterization, angioplasty, complex hybrid intervention procedures (CHIP) including rotablation, IVL; intracoronary physiology and imaging, high risk protected PCI including Impella based procedures
  3. Structural interventions – TAVI, PTMC
  4. Cardiac Devices
  5. Pacemakers – conduction system pacing, leadless pacemakers, ICD, CRT based devices
  6. Cardiac electrophysiology – EPS + RFA of both simple and complex arrhythmias, PVI for atrial fibrillation, VT ablation
  7. Advanced heart failure management including ECMO, VAD & Transplant
  8. Women's Health and preventive cardiology
